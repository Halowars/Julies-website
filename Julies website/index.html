What I Craft

Custom Gothic Garments

Bespoke corsets, dresses, and statement separates cut to your shape. Expect structured mesh, layered knits, leather panels, and hardware that feels made-for-stage but ready for every day.

  • One-of-a-kind sketches before any fabric is cut
  • Fittings available in-person or virtually
  • Hand-finished embellishments: studs, lacework, leather applique

Spellbound Jewelry

Jewelry built like armor: chokers, cuffs, and ear stacks with oxidized metals, spikes, chains, and crystals picked for your mood.

  • Mixed metals, crystals, bone, and reclaimed findings
  • Custom engraving, patinas, and mixed hardware finishes
  • Packaged with care instructions for long wear and shine

Wardrobe Alchemy

Have a piece that needs a darker life? I rework vintage finds and closet staples with over-dye, distressing, and hardware so they feel like new essentials.

  • Consultation to map the mood and materials
  • Careful preservation of sentimental elements
  • Optional styling session to finish the look

About RobotGothDoll

I am RobotGothDoll, the designer, seamstress, metalsmith, and night owl behind this studio. I draw from gothic literature, folk tales, and the sound of distant thunderstorms. Every stitch and solder is done by my hands in a studio that smells like incense and fabric dye.

My goal is to create wearable heirlooms that feel alive—pieces that empower you to show up exactly as you are. If you have a vision that feels too wild for mass-market shops, you are exactly where you need to be.
